# Netflix_Dashboard
📊 Netflix Movies & TV Shows Dashboard

This project presents an interactive data visualization dashboard analyzing Netflix’s catalog of movies and TV shows. It provides insights into content distribution, genres, ratings, and trends over time using visually rich charts.

🚀 Project Overview

The dashboard explores:

Total number of Movies vs TV Shows
Distribution of content across countries
Ratings breakdown (TV-MA, TV-14, etc.)
Top 10 genres on Netflix
Year-wise growth of content
Detailed metadata of selected titles

The goal is to uncover patterns in Netflix’s content strategy and provide an intuitive visual analysis.
🖼️ Dashboard Features
🔹 Content Distribution
Visual comparison of Movies and TV Shows
Movies dominate the platform (~68%)
🔹 Ratings Analysis
Most content falls under TV-MA and TV-14
Helps understand target audience segmentation
🔹 Global Content Map
Geographic distribution of Netflix titles
Highlights countries contributing the most content
🔹 Top Genres
Most popular genres include:
Documentaries
Stand-Up Comedy
International Movies
Dramas
🔹 Yearly Trends
Rapid growth in content after 2015
Peak content addition around 2019
